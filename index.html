About Momma Micky's Bakery

Momma Micky's started in a tiny home kitchen with one mission — to spread happiness through the art of baking. Every cake, cookie, and loaf is a labour of love, crafted with fresh local ingredients and a generous pour of heart.

Today, we serve hundreds of families across the city, bringing the same warmth and quality that Momma Micky herself put into every single batch over years.
